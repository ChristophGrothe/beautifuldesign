    • A collection of nice, beautiful things...

    Hi, I'm Chris and I am going to become a frontend developer and webdesigner. I love beautiful things and whenever I see something beautiful, I try to take a photo. However, mostly I don't have a real use for this photos. But I don't want them to be forgotten in a folder in the cloud. And so I came to the idea: Why not creating an own website for them. Maybe as kind of a artistic project. I the best case, somebody finds some inspiration looking through it.
